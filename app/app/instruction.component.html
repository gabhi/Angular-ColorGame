Instructions

The rule for this game is to tell the color of the text rather than meaning of the text.

You have 30 seconds to answer the questions. Choose the color of the text by pressing the buttons below. Wrong answer will substract the score, while correct answer incrementing score.

The buttons are intentially scrambled color.

About Color Game
Here, even the text says "Green". But the actual color of the text is red. So you want to choose the button with the text RED.
